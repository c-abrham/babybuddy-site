This page explains how to delete your Baby Go account and the data we store in the cloud for that account. Baby Go is the Android app with package name ca.babygo.app.

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

If you are signed in, you can delete your account directly in Baby Go:

  1. Open the Baby Go app on your Android device.
  2. Tap Profile in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Tap Edit profile.
  4. Scroll to the Account section (you must be signed in to see it).
  5. Tap Delete account.
  6. Tap Delete in the confirmation dialog.

Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out. You do not need to contact us for in-app deletion.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app (for example, you lost your device or cannot sign in), email us at cabraham3221@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account (or tell us the Google account you used). We will verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove from our cloud backend (Supabase):

  • Your account record (user ID and email)
  • Your synced feature reviews for places
  • Any text notes attached to those reviews
  • Your synced saved-place list

Other users will no longer see your notes in community sections. Aggregated feature counts are recalculated without your contributions.

What is not deleted automatically

The following stay on your device until you remove them yourself:

  • Local profile information (name, city, phone, profile photo)
  • Places, ratings, and notes stored only in the app’s on-device database
  • Cached Google place details (ratings, photos, addresses)

To remove local data:

  • Android Settings → Apps → Baby Go → Storage → Clear storage, or
  • Uninstall the app.

Data we never stored in the cloud

  • Your precise GPS location (used on-device and sent to Google Maps/Places only when you use those features)
  • Local profile fields (unless you separately emailed them to support)

Retention after deletion

Account data is removed from our active database when you delete your account in the app, or within 30 days of a verified email request. Backup copies held by our hosting provider may be retained for a limited period per their policies, then purged on their normal backup cycle.
