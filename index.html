About the app

Baby Buddy is an Android app for parents and caregivers. Discover restaurants, hotels, parks, and other stops with baby-friendly features — high chairs, change tables, stroller access, kids menus, and more.

Browse on a map or list, filter by what matters to your family, save favorites, and share what you find with the community when you sign in.

Features

  • Search nearby places with distance and feature filters
  • Crowdsourced baby-friendly ratings (community confirmed when enough parents agree)
  • Curated change-table data for Vancouver and Vancouver Island
  • Optional account to sync your reviews (Google or email sign-in)
  • Saved places and profile info stored on your phone
