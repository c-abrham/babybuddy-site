This Privacy Policy describes how Baby Buddy ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Baby Buddy mobile application for Android (the "Application").

Baby Buddy is a guide to baby-friendly places. Most data stays on your device. To show community-confirmed features, the app fetches anonymous, aggregated review data from our cloud provider (Supabase) when you view places — this happens even if you do not sign in. If you sign in, your own feature reviews and saved places also sync to Supabase under your account.

By using the Application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information we collect

On your device (local)

  • Optional profile: name, city, phone, and photo
  • Places database, your feature ratings, notes, and saved places
  • Filter preferences such as search distance (stored on device only, not on our servers)
  • Cached Google place details (ratings, photos, addresses) when you view places

Community data (cloud — even without an account)

When you view places, the app sends place identifiers to Supabase to load anonymous, aggregated baby-friendly feature reports from other users. This does not require an account and does not include your personal information.

When you sign in (cloud)

If you create an account with email/password or Google Sign-In:

  • Account identifier and email (from Google or your sign-up)
  • Feature reviews you submit for places
  • Saved place keys synced to your account

We use Supabase to host this data.

Location

With your permission, the app uses your location to show nearby places and center the map. Location is processed on your device and sent to Google when resolving searches or loading maps.

2. How we use information

  • Show places, maps, filters, and community-confirmed baby-friendly features
  • Store your profile, saved places, and ratings on your device
  • Sync reviews and saves when you are signed in
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data or use advertising SDKs.

3. Third-party services

  • Google Maps / Places — maps, search, and place details. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication
  • Supabase — hosts community feature data (read when you view places), and, when you sign in, your account, reviews, and saved places
  • Open data — bundled change-table maps (Vancouver Change Tables, NappyMap)

4. Your choices

  • Use the app without creating an account or signing in (your own data stays on your device; only anonymous community data is fetched)
  • Edit or delete local profile data in the app
  • Sign out or delete your account in Profile → Edit profile (see Delete account)
  • Revoke location permission in Android settings
  • Clear app storage or uninstall to remove local data

5. Children

The app is for adults (parents and caregivers), not children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
